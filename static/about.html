About Magis Air

Founded in 2022, Magis Air has served millions of people across the world with excellent customer service and dependable aircraft performance. The Magis Air Group operates more than 150 aircraft, flying to over 170 destinations worldwide. We are committed to growing our operation to help strengthen our role as one of the premier international aviation hubs.
